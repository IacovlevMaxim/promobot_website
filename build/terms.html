Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 6th, 2021

PromoBot Community Guidelines are the rules that you, as a PromoBot user, are obligated to follow when using PromoBot and PromoBot website


Community Guidelines

Accepting Discord Terms of Service

Since PromoBot is a bot on Discord, bot is obligated to follow all statements in Discord Terms of Service. Main usage rules are, but are not limited to:

  • Your server must follow all statements established by Discord, for example clean server name and server icon
  • Promo message of your server must follow all statements from Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • All actions that you do using PromoBot should not make PromoBot violate Discord Terms of Service, for example moderate command usage (not rate-limit the bot)

Exploits, bugs and errors

PromoBot may have exploits, bugs, or may throw unexpected and/or unhandled errors. If these errors or bugs lead to any type of exploits, please report them in PromoBot support server. Using the exploits for personal benefit will result in a permanent ban, meaning that you won't be able to use PromoBot in that server anymore without the right of ban appeal

User Conduct

You agree that you are responsible for everything that you transmit to or in relation to Promobot and you specifically agree (in relation to PromoBot) not to participate, spread of encourage in any form of activity which is unlawful, defamatory, libellous, abusive, vulgar, threatening, harmful, profane, obscene, harassing, sexually-oriented, racially-offensive or otherwise includes objectionable material.
You are obligated:

  • not to input or transmit content that contains software viruses or any other computer code, files, or programs that are designed or intended to disrupt, damage, or limit the functioning of any software, hardware, or telecommunications equipment or to damage or obtain unauthorised access to any data or other information of any third party;
  • not to input or transmit content that contains anything which could be used to determine or alter the architecture of PromoBot, or could be used to decompile, disassemble, or reverse engineer PromoBot;
  • not to collect personal data about other users (for any purpose);
  • not to impose an unreasonable or disproportionately large load on PromoBot infrastructure;
  • not to attempt to gain unauthorised access to PromoBot's computer systems or engage in any activity that disrupts, diminishes the quality of, interferes with the performance of, or impairs the functionality of PromoBot or PromoBot website.

If a user or a server that is using PromoBot violates one or more of these rules, this user or server will be banned from using Promobot, and depending on the severity of the violation, this ban may or may not be appealable.
